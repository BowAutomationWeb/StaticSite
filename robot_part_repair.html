Robot Part Repair

Need to have a part repaired or a complete robot refurbished like new?
Bow Robotics can help!

The Bow Robotics team has engineers versed in electrical, mechanical, and software applications. We can quickly and accurately assess the issues to identify the root problem of the specific part or function. Once the problem has been identified; we fix and/or replace, clean, and prep your part for testing. Our testing process usually involves manual circuit and/or manipulations checks along with the part running through various test simulations on one of our test robots for at least 24 hours, and are backed by a 12-month warranty unless otherwise stated.
